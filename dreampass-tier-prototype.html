Value-at-Stake Paywall Design
Paywalls that show a concrete "value delivered" figure (AED 1,320 in benefits vs AED 499 price) outperform feature-list paywalls by 31% on conversion. ROI framing reduces price anchoring resistance.
Hotjar Paywall UX Study 2023; CXL Institute Subscription Conversion 2023

Paid travel members who use a benefit in their first trip post-upgrade renew at 76% vs 38% for those who don't use a benefit in the first trip. Lounge access is the highest-engagement first benefit.
