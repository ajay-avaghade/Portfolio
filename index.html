Virtusa — Merchant Acquiring

Virtusa is building next-gen payment infrastructure for merchant acquiring in MENA. I identified the terminal churn problem — 30% of deployed POS devices go inactive within 90 days — and designed a milestone-based rental waiver to drive 90-day stickiness, built on a program I shipped at PhonePe.

Merchant Stickiness

MerchantOS: Milestone Rental Waiver

30% of deployed POS terminals go inactive within 90 days. Flat discounts burn CAPEX with no behaviour change. This milestone system waives the AED 45/month rental to AED 1 when merchants hit TPV + TPC goals — I built this mechanic end-to-end at PhonePe, A/B tested it against cashback, and it drove 34% higher goal completion.

↑ 60% 90-day active device rate
