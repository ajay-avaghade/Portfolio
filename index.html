amazon pay

Amazon — Payments & Emerging Stores

Amazon's Emerging Stores Payments team owns the payments journey across MENA and APAC — including instant bank discount (IBD) programs that run at hundreds of crores per year. I built two case studies targeting Amazon's core IBD problem: precision discount surfacing via ML propensity scoring, and a habit-forming milestone tier program funded by bank partners.

Payments · ML

SmartOffer Engine

Amazon's IBD budget shows the same bank discount banner to every checkout user — high-intent buyers who'd convert anyway drain 40–60% of the budget with zero incremental GMV. SmartOffer uses a Propensity-to-Convert ML model to detect undecided users at checkout entry and surface the bank offer only to them, turning IBD from a spray-and-pray banner into a precision conversion lever.

30–35% IBD budget leakage reduction
18–22% Conversion lift, undecided cohort
3.2× IBD ROI improvement

Payments · Retention

Amazon Pay Milestone

60–70% of IBD-activated users make only one transaction in 90 days. The discount is a one-time event, not a habit. Milestone introduces progressive tier rewards (5% → 8% → 12% off) earned by transacting with the linked bank card across any Amazon Pay surface — shopping, bills, travel. Bank partners fund the escalating tiers; Amazon gets retention data and repeat GMV at zero discount cost.
